# Return Procedure — Electronics Service
**Document version**: 1.0 (example / MVP seed)
**Applies to**: All consumer electronics categories listed in the product categories list.

---

## 1. General Eligibility Rules

**RULE-R-01** — A return is eligible for processing if submitted within **30 calendar days** of the purchase date. Returns submitted after 30 days are rejected.

**RULE-R-02** — A return requires photographic evidence showing the equipment's current physical condition. A single image is required.

**RULE-R-03** — For a return to be accepted, the item must be in a condition that allows it to be resold as new or like-new. The item must show no signs of use beyond opening the packaging.

---

## 2. Condition Classification

**RULE-R-04** — Item condition is classified into one of three categories:

| Classification | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Unopened / Like-new** | Original packaging intact or re-sealable, no signs of use, all original accessories and documentation present |
| **Used** | Visible signs of use: scratches, fingerprints on non-packaging surfaces, missing stickers, activated accounts, paired devices |
| **Damaged** | Physical damage present: cracks, dents, broken components, liquid damage |

**RULE-R-05** — Only items classified as **Unopened / Like-new** are eligible for acceptance.

**RULE-R-06** — Items classified as **Used** are rejected. Customer must be informed of the specific signs of use identified.

**RULE-R-07** — Items classified as **Damaged** are rejected. If the damage occurred after purchase, the customer should be directed to the complaints procedure (RULE-C series).

---

## 3. Specific Equipment Rules

**RULE-R-08 — Activated devices** (smartphones, tablets, laptops): If the device has been powered on and set up (accounts configured, device paired), it is classified as Used regardless of physical appearance.

**RULE-R-09 — Software**: If a software license or activation code included with the product has been used or redeemed, the return is rejected regardless of physical condition.

**RULE-R-10 — Hygiene items** (in-ear headphones, certain wearables): Returns are not accepted once packaging is opened, for hygiene reasons.

**RULE-R-11 — Bundled products**: All components of a bundle (e.g. phone + charger + case) must be present for the return to be accepted. Missing components result in rejection or partial deduction (partial deduction is not available in MVP — reject and inform customer).

**RULE-R-12 — Large appliances**: Returns for items in the "Małe AGD" category require the original packaging to be present and undamaged. A missing or damaged box results in rejection.

---

## 4. Decision Rules

**RULE-R-13** — Accept the return if ALL of the following are true:
- Purchase date is within 30 calendar days of submission date.
- Image analysis confirms no visible signs of use or damage.
- Item category is not subject to a hygiene-based no-return rule (RULE-R-10).

**RULE-R-14** — Reject the return if ANY of the following are true:
- Purchase date exceeds 30 calendar days.
- Image analysis identifies visible signs of use (scratches, activation indicators, worn surfaces).
- Image analysis identifies physical damage.
- Item falls under RULE-R-09 (used software licence) or RULE-R-10 (hygiene item, opened).

**RULE-R-15** — Issue a "Wymaga weryfikacji" (Requires verification) decision if:
- Image is ambiguous and cannot confirm or exclude signs of use.
- The image shows the packaging but the equipment itself is not clearly visible.
- The case falls into a category not covered by rules above.

---

## 5. Next Steps by Decision

| Decision | Next Steps for Employee |
|---|---|
| **Zaakceptowano** | Process the return in the POS/service system. Issue a return confirmation number. Arrange equipment collection or accept in-store. Inform the customer that the refund will be processed within 5 business days to the original payment method. |
| **Odrzucono** | Communicate the rejection reason to the customer, citing the specific rule (e.g. "item shows signs of use per RULE-R-06"). Return the item to the customer. Inform the customer they may submit a complaint if they believe there is a manufacturing defect. |
| **Wymaga weryfikacji** | Escalate the case to the service supervisor with a full case summary and image. Do not accept or reject unilaterally. Inform the customer the case is under review and they will be contacted within 1 business day. |
